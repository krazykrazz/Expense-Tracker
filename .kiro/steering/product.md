# Product Overview

A full-stack personal expense tracking application for managing household finances. Users can add, edit, and delete expenses with categorization by type (Food, Gas, Other, Tax-deductible items) and payment method. The app provides monthly and annual summaries, tracks gross income vs expenses, supports recurring expense templates, and manages fixed monthly expenses. Features include CSV import/export, automated database backups, and tax-deductible expense reporting for medical and donation categories.

## Key Features

- Expense management with search and filtering
- Monthly and annual financial summaries with weekly breakdowns
- Configurable monthly gross income from multiple sources
- Fixed monthly expenses management with carry-forward capability
- Recurring expense templates with automatic generation
- Tax-deductible expense tracking (medical and donations)
- CSV import for bulk expense entry
- Automated and manual database backups
- Local network access for multi-device usage
